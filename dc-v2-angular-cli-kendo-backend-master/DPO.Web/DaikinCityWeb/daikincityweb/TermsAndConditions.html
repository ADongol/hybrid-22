What are cookies and how does Daikin North America LLC use them?

Daikin North America LLC uses "cookie" technology to obtain non-personal information from online visitors. A "cookie" is a small text file that is sent to your web browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Daikin North America LLC uses "cookies" as a means of compiling information detailing the usage of our site. These findings help us understand our customers’ needs so that we may improve your browsing/shopping experience at Daikin North America LLC.

Generally, we may use "cookies" to:

  • Estimate the number of users of our site
  • Quantify general usage of our site
  • Track aggregate traffic patterns
  • Assist in the personalization and general improvement of your experience at Daikin North America, LLC

Personal information cannot be extracted by simply placing a "cookie" on an individual’s computer. If you have submitted personal information to Daikin North America LLC, then the aggregated data may be combined with some personal information for internal marketing and reporting purposes.

Even though most Web browsers accept "cookies", most browsers also give you the option to reject "cookies". Please note that if you set your browser to reject "cookies" you will still be able to visit the Daikin North America LLC site but your shopping and browsing experience may be somewhat affected.
